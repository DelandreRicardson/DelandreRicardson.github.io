Details about applications

There are three positions that you can apply for at Camp Canada. There are general camp counselor, activities camp counselor ans support staff. General camp counselors manage and guides the course of the camp. Activities camp counselors creates and manages the activities that happens within the camp. Support Staff helps out with overlooking the camp, the other positions and the people participating.
